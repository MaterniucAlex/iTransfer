Collaborative solutions for the community

THE GREAT HALL OF THE IASI TOWN HALL

Collaborative Solutions for the Community is an initiative of the Civic Association, through which the people of Iași can decide the city’s priorities in 7 areas of development. The actors involved in this initiative are represented by the executive team, experts from various fields, mentoring organizations, multidisciplinary teams, partners and an advisory board.

The iTransfer mission is to stimulate innovation and technology transfer in order to capitalize on research results by introducing them into the economic circuit in the form of innovative products and services.
