The Significance of Networking for Career Advancement

ROOM C308, BUILDING C, FACULTY OF COMPUTER SCIENCE, ALEXANDRU IOAN CUZA UNIVERSITY

The lecture given by Dr. Dorina Roșca deepened the critical role that networking plays in the development of a successful professional career. The presentation focused on the advantages of networking, techniques for creating a valuable professional network, and ways to make the most of networking opportunities. Dr. Dorina Roșca is a researcher and president of the Institut Européen d’Etudes du Développement (IEED), as well as an associate researcher at Ladyss, UMR 7533 CNRS. He is co-coordinator of the Hespri Project, a Horizon Europe personnel exchange program coordinated by UAIC.

Open Science, Open Data and Open Access: a focus on research for EU project

ROOM C413, BUILDING C, FACULTY OF COMPUTER SCIENCE, ALEXANDRU IOAN CUZA UNIVERSITY OF IASI

The lecture given by Dr. Valentina Goglio, assistant professor of Economic Sociology, University of Turin (Italy), provided an overview of the concept and the different terms that make up the Open Science ecosystem and focused on the different stages in which the principles of Open Science can be included in the research design. The broad concept of Open Science is gaining momentum in recent years, however its origin is rooted in movements that spread decades ago in the field of computer science (the open software and open source movement) or education (the open education movement). Open Science became now a keyword as institutional funders, such as the European Commission, require compliance with at least some of its basic principles for any research project submitted, e.g. in the framework of the Horizon Europe program.
